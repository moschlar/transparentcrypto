transparentcrypto
Advanced usability/visibility for PGP cryptography in Emails to support cryptography literacy.

This Thunderbird addon consists of two parts: An Email Encryption Live Preview window while composing a message and a Graph Visualization of your PGP Keyring.

Keyring Graph

You can use your mouse to navigate the Keyring Graph by just clicking dragging the canvas around and zooming with your mouse wheel. You can also move nodes horizontally.

Used Software

  • openpgp.js
  • async.js
  • vis.js
  • Colorbrewer (Javascript files from d3.js)
